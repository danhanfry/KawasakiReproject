handling
New Chassis
New chassis with a trellis frame is lighter and slimmer with improved rigidity. The result is light and nimble handling and a more direct connection to the road.
New Overall Weight
The 2017 Ninja® 650 sportbike weighs significantly less than the previous model. This results in light handling and easy maneuverability.
New Horizontal Back-Link Rear Suspension
The horizontal back-link rear suspension derived from World Superbike Championship Ninja® technology contributes to improved traction, response and ride comfort. A balance of sporty performance and everyday comfort is achieved.
New 5-Spoke Wheels
Rigid lightweight wheels contribute to nimble handling and sporty styling.
Under-Engine Exhaust
Placement of the exhaust under the engine contributes to lower weight centralization for nimble handling.
New Nissin® Brake Calipers
Lightweight Nissin brake calipers contribute to strong and responsive stopping power.
New Gull-Arm Design Swingarm
The new gull-arm design swingarm provides a lower center of gravity and makes room for the under-engine exhaust, resulting in nimble handling and increased maneuverability.
