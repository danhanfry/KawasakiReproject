Ride Position
An upright riding position offers relaxed, sporty ergonomics allowing riders to fully capitalize on the sporty street riding potential of the Ninja® 650 sportbike.
New Seat Height
Reduced seat height along with the bike’s slim overall design makes it easy to keep both feet firmly planted on the ground when stopped. It accommodates a wide range of rider sizes and promotes confidence when riding.
New Handlebars
Clip-on style handlebars promote an aggressive Ninja sportbike image while complementing the ergonomics of the upright seating position of the Ninja sportbike.
New Footpeg Position
Footpegs have been repositioned for improved ergonomics. This positioning offers a more relaxed and comfortable ride position.
